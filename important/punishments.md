# Punishments

{% hint style="info" %}
To appeal a punishment, please see [this section](../info/rules/#appeals).&#x20;
{% endhint %}

## Ban-able Offenses:

### Cheating

Cheating refers to the use of any blacklisted modification (both for combat and non combat) on the server. This includes hack clients, along with macros and auto-clickers. _Utilization of cheats to gain items may result in an_ [_account wipe_](punishments.md#wipe)_._

#### Punishment Duration:

* 1st Offense
  * 30 day ban
* 2nd Offense
  * 6 month ban
* 3rd Offense
  * Permanent ban

### X-Ray&#x20;

This punishment refers to the usage of mods / texture packs that allow you to see things you wouldn't otherwise be able to see. Mods such as ESP & Base Finder are categorized under this section. _Utilization of X-Ray to gain items /_ _raid bases may result in an_ [_account wipe_](punishments.md#wipe)_._ Vanilla (built into the game) base finding features are allowed.

#### Punishment Duration:

* 1st Offense
  * 14 day ban
* 2nd Offense
  * 60 day ban
* 3rd Offense
  * Permanent ban

### Bug Abuse&#x20;

This punishment refers to the exploitation of bugs / glitches for personal gain or to damage the server. Bugs such as dupe glitches, infinite money glitches, and server crashes all fall under this section. _Utilizing dupe /_ _infinite money glitches will result in an_ [_account wipe_](punishments.md#wipe)_._

#### Punishment Duration:

* 1st Offense
  * 7 day ban
* 2nd Offense
  * 60 day ban
* 3rd Offense
  * Permanent ban

### Teleport Trapping / Killing

This punishment refers to the action of teleport trapping or teleport killing.. _Utilizing dupe /_ _infinite money glitches will result in an_ [_account wipe_](punishments.md#wipe)_._

#### Punishment Duration:

* 1st Offense
  * 7 day ban
* 2nd Offense
  * 60 day ban
* 3rd Offense
  * Permanent ban

## Mute-able Offenses

### Spam&#x20;

This punishment refers to the sending of repeated or unwanted messages via the chat.

#### Punishment Duration:

* 1st Offense
  * 3 hour mute
* 2nd Offense
  * 1 day mute
* 3rd Offense
  * 1 week mute
* 4th Offense
  * 3 month mute

### Inappropriate Language

This punishment refers to the sending of inappropriate language that is disallowed within our community.

#### Punishment Duration:

* 1st Offense
  * 5 hour mute
* 2nd Offense
  * 3 day mute
* 3rd Offense
  * 2 week mute
* 4th Offense
  * 3 month mute

### Disrespect

This punishment refers to the sending of disrespectful or upsetting messages.

#### Punishment Duration:

* 1st Offense
  * 1 day mute
* 2nd Offense
  * 1 week mute
* 3rd Offense
  * 3 month mute

### Advertising

This punishment refers to the advertising of external services / servers or social media.

#### Punishment Duration:

* 1st Offense
  * 1 day mute
* 2nd Offense
  * 1 week mute
* 3rd Offense
  * 3 month mute

### Extreme Chat Offense

This punishment includes the usage of slurs, doxxing threats, IRL threats, and other severe chat offenses. Depending on the severity of the offense, one may be permanently banned for a singular offense.

#### Punishment Duration:

* 1st Offense
  * 1 month mute - permanent ban
* 2nd Offense
  * Permanent ban

## Punishment Types

### Ban

This punishment is a restriction placed upon  your account which disallows said account from joining the server.

### Mute

This punishment is a blockage from sending chat messages within the server, along with using certain commands such as /msg.&#x20;

### Blacklist

Also known as an IP-Ban, this is a restriction placed upon your whole network that disallows all accounts on said network from joining the server.

### Wipe

This is a total reset of an account on the server.&#x20;

A wipe resets a player's:

* Balance
* Inventory
* Ender Chest
* Homes
* Stats (Kills & Deaths)
* Bases (Situational)

### Warn

This is an official warning issued to a player. It comes with no consequences by itself, however having warnings may affect one's ability to become staff and may result in longer punishments for other offenses.&#x20;
