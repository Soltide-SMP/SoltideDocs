# Battle Arena

## How to access the arena

You can access the arena via the `/warp` command by selecting the `Diamond Sword`.

## Arena info

* Going too far from the arena will damage you.
* You can place both water and cobwebs within the arena.
* You receive `7%` of the balance of the person you've killed.
  * A 5% tax is applied to all earnings from the arena, so you'll gain `95%` of the `7%`.
  * The person killed loses `7%` of their balance.
* Maces are allowed.
* There is a `7 second` cool down for all teleports out of the arena.
